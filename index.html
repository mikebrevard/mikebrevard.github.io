Calistoga Spa Hot Springs

1006 Washington St Calistoga, CA

Enjoy mineral hot springs and a spa treatment before our big day. Or just enjoy the sun poolside!

Indian Springs

1712 Lincoln Calistoga, CA

Come and stay at the Indian Springs and be transported back to the elegance of a 1920's resort.

The Bergson

1010 Foothill Blvd Calistoga, CA

Stay right near dowtown Callistoga in a quaint hotel with the option to sip wine fireside in your own room!

Calistoga Inn

1250 Lincoln Ave Calistoga, CA

Enjoy the restaurant or brewery just downstairs or chose from a number of restaurants nearby with this conveniently placed inn.

Craftman Bungalow

Calistoga, CA

This eclectic and charming bugalow can fit up to 4 guests. It has 2 beds and 1 bath and a five star rating.

Barn Style Studio

Calistoga, CA

This barn style studio has 1 bedroom and 1 bath. It has a 5-star rating and a full kitche. Enjoy a little bit of nature while staying in this home away from home.
